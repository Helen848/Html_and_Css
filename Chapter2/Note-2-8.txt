  * Section 2.8 Whitespace Collapsing
  
 - Whitespace refers to indivisible charactors displayed in a browser.
 - The comman exaples are blank spaces, newline and tab characters, generated by pressing spacebar, enter and tab keys, respectively.
 - Browsers, collapse whitespace is in your source code if you have consecutive blank spaces, newlines, tabs the browse will only display as one whitespace on the web page.
 - Line wrap is when a word bumps up against the right margin and is automatically moved to the next line.
